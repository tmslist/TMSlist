## Anthem BCBS and TMS coverage

Anthem Blue Cross Blue Shield covers TMS therapy for treatment-resistant major depressive disorder across most of its commercial and Medicare Advantage plans. As one of the largest BCBS licensees, Anthem's policies apply to millions of members in 14+ states.

## Eligibility criteria

Anthem generally requires:

1. **Diagnosis:** Major Depressive Disorder (MDD), moderate to severe
2. **Medication trials:** At least 2 adequate antidepressant trials from different classes
3. **Adequate trial definition:** Therapeutic dose for minimum 6 weeks each
4. **Documentation:** PHQ-9 or equivalent depression rating scale, plus clinical notes from treating psychiatrist
5. **Prior authorization:** Required before starting treatment

## What's typically covered

- **Initial course:** Up to 36 sessions of rTMS (including theta burst protocols)
- **Maintenance:** Coverage for maintenance sessions varies by plan — check your specific benefits
- **Any FDA-cleared device:** NeuroStar, BrainsWay, MagVenture, etc.

## Cost sharing by plan type

- **Anthem PPO:** Deductible + coinsurance (typically 20% in-network)
- **Anthem HMO:** Specialist copay per visit ($30-$60 typical)
- **Anthem Medicare Advantage:** Part B specialist copay
- **High-deductible (HDHP):** Full cost until deductible met, then coinsurance

## Prior authorization tips

1. Your TMS clinic typically submits the prior auth request
2. Include: detailed medication history with doses/durations/reasons for failure, current PHQ-9 score, psychiatrist letter of medical necessity
3. **Response time:** 5-15 business days for standard requests; expedited review available for urgent cases
4. **Pre-service vs concurrent:** Initial auth covers the full treatment course; some plans require mid-course review at session 18-20

## If denied

- **Most common denial reasons:** Insufficient documentation of medication trials, missing depression severity scores, or failure to meet "adequate trial" criteria
- **Peer-to-peer review:** Your psychiatrist can speak directly with Anthem's medical reviewer — this often resolves denials
- **Formal appeal:** Submit within 180 days with additional documentation
- **External review:** Available if internal appeal fails

## Finding in-network providers

- Use Anthem's "Find Care" tool at anthem.com
- Search for "TMS" or "transcranial magnetic stimulation"
- Filter by your specific plan
- Verify directly with the TMS clinic that they accept your Anthem plan (network status can change)
