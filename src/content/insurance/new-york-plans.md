## TMS coverage in New York

New York has strong mental health parity laws that benefit TMS patients. Timothy's Law (2006) and subsequent regulations require commercial health plans to cover mental health services comparably to medical/surgical benefits. This means most NY health plans cover TMS for treatment-resistant depression.

## Major NY plans and TMS

**Empire Blue Cross Blue Shield:**
- Largest commercial plan in NY
- Covers TMS with prior authorization
- Follows national BCBS TMS medical policy
- Strong provider network in NYC metro, Long Island, and upstate

**EmblemHealth (GHI/HIP):**
- Major NYC-area plan, especially for municipal workers
- Covers TMS for treatment-resistant MDD
- Prior authorization through behavioral health department
- Network includes TMS clinics across all 5 boroughs

**Fidelis Care:**
- Managed Medicaid and Essential Plan
- TMS coverage varies by plan — check directly
- Growing provider partnerships for TMS access

**Oscar Health:**
- ACA marketplace plan popular in NYC
- Covers TMS with prior authorization
- Virtual-first model — psychiatrist visit may be telehealth, but TMS is in-person

**1199SEIU:**
- Major healthcare workers union plan
- Covers TMS through Empire BCBS network
- Contact benefits office for specific authorization requirements

## New York Medicaid

New York Medicaid covers TMS therapy through its managed care plans:
- Requires prior authorization
- Must be provided at Medicaid-enrolled facility
- Available in NYC and increasingly upstate
- Medicaid managed care plans (Fidelis, Healthfirst, MetroPlus, Affinity) each have their own TMS policies

## NY-specific advantages

**Mental health parity:**
- Timothy's Law requires equal coverage for mental health
- If your plan covers medical devices for other conditions, it must cover TMS equivalently
- NY Department of Financial Services (DFS) enforces parity violations

**Surprise billing protection:**
- NY's surprise billing law protects you from unexpected out-of-network charges
- If your in-network TMS clinic uses an out-of-network provider, you're held harmless

## NYC TMS landscape

New York City has one of the densest concentrations of TMS providers nationally:
- **Manhattan:** 30+ TMS clinics
- **Brooklyn:** 10+ providers
- **Queens:** Growing availability
- **Bronx, Staten Island:** Limited but expanding
- **Long Island:** 15+ providers
- **Westchester/Hudson Valley:** 10+ providers

## Tips for NY patients

- New York's parity laws are strong — if TMS is denied, cite Timothy's Law in your appeal
- NY DFS has a dedicated consumer complaint line for insurance disputes
- Many NYC TMS clinics accept multiple insurance plans — call to verify yours
- Consider both Manhattan and outer borough clinics — prices and wait times vary
- If using the NY State of Health marketplace plan, confirm TMS is covered under your specific tier
