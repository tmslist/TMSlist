## Does Humana cover TMS therapy?

Yes. Humana covers TMS therapy for treatment-resistant depression under most of its commercial and Medicare Advantage plans. Coverage requires prior authorization and documented medication failures.

## Eligibility requirements

Humana's medical policy for TMS typically requires:

1. **Diagnosis:** Major Depressive Disorder (MDD), moderate to severe
2. **Medication failures:** At least 2 adequate antidepressant trials
   - Different drug classes (e.g., one SSRI + one SNRI)
   - Adequate dose for at least 6 weeks each
   - Documented reason for failure (lack of response or intolerable side effects)
3. **Psychiatric evaluation:** Current assessment by a psychiatrist
4. **PHQ-9 score:** Documenting moderate or severe depression (typically score 10+)
5. **No contraindications:** No metallic implants near treatment site, no seizure disorder

## What's covered

- **Sessions:** Up to 36 sessions for initial treatment course
- **Maintenance:** Some plans cover maintenance sessions (up to 12 per year) — verify with your specific plan
- **Device:** Coverage applies regardless of specific TMS device (NeuroStar, BrainsWay, MagVenture, etc.)

## Prior authorization process

1. Your TMS clinic submits a prior authorization request to Humana
2. Include: psychiatrist notes, medication trial history, PHQ-9 scores, treatment plan
3. **Timeline:** Humana typically responds within 5-15 business days
4. If approved, you'll receive a reference number for your TMS sessions
5. Treatment must begin within 60 days of authorization

## Cost sharing

Your out-of-pocket costs depend on your specific Humana plan:

- **Humana Gold Plus (Medicare Advantage):** Specialist copay per visit ($30-$50 typical)
- **Humana Choice PPO:** Deductible + coinsurance (usually 20-30% for in-network)
- **Humana HMO:** Specialist copay; referral from PCP required
- **High-deductible plans:** Full cost until deductible met, then coinsurance

All TMS costs count toward your annual out-of-pocket maximum.

## Finding in-network providers

- Use Humana's "Find a Doctor" tool and search for "TMS" or "transcranial magnetic stimulation"
- Call the TMS clinic directly to confirm they accept your specific Humana plan
- In-network providers save you significantly vs out-of-network (especially for 36 sessions)
- If no in-network providers in your area, request a network exception from Humana

## If denied

1. **Request the denial reason in writing** — Humana must explain specifically why
2. **Common denial reasons:** Insufficient medication trial documentation, missing psychiatric evaluation, or coding errors
3. **Peer-to-peer review:** Your psychiatrist can speak directly with Humana's medical reviewer
4. **Formal appeal:** Submit additional documentation addressing the denial reason
5. **External review:** If internal appeal fails, you can request independent external review
6. **Timing:** File appeals within 180 days of denial

## Tips for Humana members

- Ask your TMS clinic if they have experience with Humana authorizations — experienced clinics have higher approval rates
- Keep copies of all medication records, therapy notes, and PHQ-9 scores
- Start the authorization process 2-3 weeks before you want to begin treatment
- Confirm your plan's specific cost sharing before starting — call the number on your Humana card
