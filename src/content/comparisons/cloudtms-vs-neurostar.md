CloudTMS and NeuroStar both use figure-8 coil technology and are FDA-cleared for depression treatment. The real difference is philosophy: NeuroStar built its system around clinical workflow and outcomes tracking. CloudTMS built its system around cloud connectivity and multi-site analytics. For patients, both deliver equivalent care. For clinic operators, the difference matters more.

## What You'll Learn

- Technical and platform differences
- Cloud connectivity and analytics advantages
- US availability
- Insurance and cost considerations

## Technical Platforms

**NeuroStar Advanced Therapy:**
- Proprietary iron-core figure-8 coil with contact sensing
- TrakStar data management (local + cloud sync)
- Express TMS protocol (19-minute theta burst)
- Largest clinical evidence base from 1M+ treatments logged
- Most widely installed system in the US

**CloudTMS:**
- Figure-8 coil design with a cloud-native platform
- Real-time treatment data streaming to a cloud dashboard
- Multi-provider analytics and benchmarking across sites
- Supports standard and theta burst protocols
- Built for multi-location clinic management from the ground up

## The Cloud Advantage

CloudTMS's core differentiation is data infrastructure:
- Real-time treatment analytics across all locations
- Outcome tracking and benchmarking for practice management
- Standardized protocol delivery across multi-site operations
- Modern pricing models for smaller practices

For patients, this doesn't affect treatment quality — it affects how well the practice manages your care data. For clinic operators, it's a different business tool.

## Clinical Equivalence

Both use similar figure-8 coil technology and support equivalent protocols:
- Response rates: 50-60%
- Remission rates: 30-35%
- Both offer standard and theta burst sessions

The physics of figure-8 coil stimulation are the same regardless of the brand on the machine.

## US Availability

NeuroStar is installed at 1,000+ US locations — finding a provider is straightforward in most areas. CloudTMS is growing but from a smaller base, primarily in practices that specifically chose it for its cloud analytics features.

## For Patients

Your outcome depends on your provider's expertise, not which device they own. Both are FDA-cleared and evidence-based. The cloud connectivity that differentiates CloudTMS is a practice management feature — it doesn't change what happens during your treatment session.
