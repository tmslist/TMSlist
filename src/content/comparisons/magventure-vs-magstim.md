MagVenture and Magstim are both rooted in European TMS tradition. MagVenture is a Danish-Norwegian company; Magstim is UK-based. Both built figure-8 coil systems, both have strong research pedigrees, and both appear frequently in published TMS literature. The comparison in the US market is less about technical differences and more about availability and which clinic uses which system.

## What You'll Learn

- Technical similarities and differences between the two platforms
- Protocol support on each system
- US availability and insurance landscape
- Which scenarios favor one over the other

## Technical Comparison

Both systems use figure-8 coil designs and support the same core clinical protocols. The technical philosophy is similar — both are conventional coil platforms optimized for reproducible, reliable stimulation of the prefrontal cortex.

MagVenture has iterated its coil designs for different clinical applications, including specific coils for OCD and smoking cessation protocols. Magstim's Rapid² is known for flexibility in coil configurations and is widely used in research settings that require precise stimulation parameters.

For standard depression treatment, both deliver equivalent outcomes. The difference for most patients is not clinical but logistical: what does your clinic use, and what's covered by your insurance.

## FDA Clearances

Both systems hold FDA clearance for treatment-resistant major depressive disorder. The specific clearances and configurations vary, but the core indication is shared.

For OCD, both can be used off-label. Neither has the dedicated OCD indication that BrainsWay holds, though some research has used both systems for OCD trials.

## US Availability

This is where the two diverge. MagVenture has become more widely adopted in US private practice and hospital settings, particularly in academic medical centers. Magstim is common in research hospitals and academic TMS centers but has a smaller commercial US footprint than MagVenture.

For most patients, finding a MagVenture provider is more straightforward than finding a Magstim provider in the US.

For depression treatment, MagVenture and Magstim are clinically equivalent. Choose based on availability and insurance coverage at your local clinic. Explore our [provider directory](/us/) to see what's available near you, or compare other [TMS devices](/compare/).
