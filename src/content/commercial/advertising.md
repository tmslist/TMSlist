## Advertise to TMS Patients and Providers

TMS List reaches thousands of patients and providers searching for TMS therapy every month. These aren't casual browsers. They're actively looking for treatment — and that makes them the right audience for your message.

## Who Should Advertise

- **TMS clinics** that want more patients in their market
- **Device manufacturers** getting their TMS systems in front of clinics and patients
- **Healthcare financing companies** offering payment plans for TMS
- **Pharmaceutical companies** with products that complement TMS
- **Medical staffing firms** recruiting TMS technicians and psychiatrists

## Advertising Options

### Sponsored Clinic Listings
Your clinic shows up first. When someone searches for TMS providers in your area, your listing sits at the top of the city and state pages with a "Sponsored" label. Simple. Effective.

### Display Advertising
Banner placements on our highest-traffic pages — treatment guides, insurance pages, and the clinic directory. Standard IAB sizes available.

### Content Sponsorship
Sponsor an educational article or treatment guide. Your brand appears alongside genuinely useful content that helps people make treatment decisions. Good for you, good for them.

### Email Newsletter
Get in front of our subscriber base of patients and providers. Dedicated email placement or newsletter sponsorship — your call.

## Our Standards

We keep it clean:

- All advertising is clearly labeled as sponsored
- Paid placements never touch our editorial content or clinic rankings
- We vet every advertiser to confirm they offer legitimate TMS services
- No space for unproven or fraudulent treatments. Period.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What does advertising on TMS List cost?
Pricing varies by format and placement. Sponsored clinic listings start at a monthly rate and scale with your target markets. Display advertising and content sponsorship are priced based on reach and page context. Contact us at advertise@tmslist.com for a custom quote.

### Does advertising affect clinic rankings on TMS List?
No. Rankings on TMS List are based purely on verified patient reviews, clinic data completeness, and clinical credentials. Advertising never influences where your clinic appears in organic search results or our comparison tools.

### How do I get started with advertising?
Reach out to our partnerships team at advertise@tmslist.com or visit our contact page to discuss your goals and which advertising format fits best. We typically respond within one business day.
