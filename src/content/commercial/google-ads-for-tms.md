## Paid Search for TMS: Quick Results, Real Patients

SEO takes months. Google Ads puts you at the top of search results today. And for TMS, paid search works especially well — the people searching have already decided they want TMS. They just need a provider.

## Expected Costs and ROI

### Cost Per Click (CPC)
TMS keywords typically run:
- "TMS therapy near me" — $15–$40 per click
- "TMS for depression [city]" — $10–$30 per click
- "TMS clinic [city]" — $8–$25 per click
- "Does insurance cover TMS?" — $5–$15 per click

### Conversion Metrics
What you can realistically expect:
- **Click-through rate:** 3–8% (search ads)
- **Landing page conversion rate:** 5–15% (phone call or form fill)
- **Lead to patient conversion:** 20–40%
- **Cost per new patient:** $200–$800

### ROI Calculation
A full TMS treatment course brings in $6,000–$15,000. If your cost per acquired patient is $400, that's a 15–37x return. Hard to beat.

## Campaign Structure

### Campaign 1: Brand Keywords
- Your clinic name + "TMS"
- Low CPC, high conversion rate
- Keeps competitors from poaching patients searching for you by name

### Campaign 2: Local Treatment Keywords
- "[City] TMS therapy"
- "TMS for depression [city]"
- "TMS clinic near me" (with location targeting)
- Highest intent. Highest conversion.

### Campaign 3: Condition Keywords
- "Treatment-resistant depression treatment"
- "TMS for OCD"
- "TMS for anxiety"
- Broader reach, lower intent, cheaper clicks

### Campaign 4: Insurance Keywords
- "[Carrier] TMS coverage"
- "Does [carrier] cover TMS?"
- Catches people who already want TMS and are checking if their plan pays for it

## Ad Copy Best Practices

### What Works
- Mention insurance: "Most Insurance Accepted"
- Give them a next step: "Free Consultation" or "Call Today"
- Use location: "TMS Therapy in [City]"
- Show credentials: "Board-Certified Psychiatrists"
- Create urgency: "Now Accepting New Patients"

### What Doesn't Work
- Vague healthcare fluff: "We care about your wellbeing"
- Technical jargon: "Repetitive transcranial magnetic stimulation protocols"
- Competing on price — it signals low quality

## Landing Pages

Never send ad traffic to your homepage. Build dedicated landing pages with:
- A clear headline that matches what they searched
- Phone number front and center
- Simple form (name, phone, insurance carrier)
- Brief TMS explanation
- Insurance logos
- Patient testimonials
- Location and hours

## Budget Allocation

For a single-location TMS clinic, start here:
- **Monthly budget:** $2,000–$5,000
- **Split:** 60% local keywords, 25% condition keywords, 15% brand protection
- **Commit to 3 months** before judging ROI — TMS has a long patient journey from first click to treatment start

Once you see which keywords and ads produce actual patients (not just clicks), scale up the winners.
