## List Your TMS Clinic

TMS List is the largest directory of TMS therapy providers worldwide. People come here to find clinics, compare providers, and decide where to get treatment. A listing puts your practice directly in front of someone actively searching for TMS.

## What You Get

### Free Basic Listing
- Clinic name, address, and contact info
- Phone number and website link
- TMS devices used
- Insurance carriers accepted
- Patient reviews and ratings
- Map placement in your city and state

### Verified Listing (Free)
Everything in the basic listing, plus:
- A "Verified" badge on your profile
- Priority placement in search results
- Ability to respond to patient reviews
- Analytics dashboard showing profile views and clicks

### Premium Listing
Everything in verified, plus:
- Featured spot at the top of your city/state page
- Custom clinic description and provider bios
- Photo gallery (facility, treatment rooms, your team)
- Direct booking link or inquiry form
- Monthly performance report
- A dedicated account manager

## How to Get Listed

1. **Go to [Claim Your Listing](/claim-listing/)** and search for your clinic
2. If we already have your clinic, claim it by verifying ownership
3. If we don't, submit your clinic information
4. Our team reviews submissions within 2 business days
5. You're live

## Verification Process

To earn the "Verified" badge, we confirm:
- Your clinic is a real, operating TMS practice
- The address matches your physical location
- A licensed psychiatrist or physician is tied to the practice
- Your TMS device is FDA-cleared

## Why Patients Trust TMS List

- We don't take payment to influence rankings
- Reviews come from real patients (verified through our review system)
- Clinic information gets regularly updated and fact-checked
- Any paid placements or sponsored content are clearly disclosed
