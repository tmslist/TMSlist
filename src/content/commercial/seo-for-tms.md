## Why SEO Matters for TMS Clinics

When someone searches "TMS therapy near me" or "TMS for depression [city]," they're ready to act. Ranking on page one for those searches means a steady stream of qualified patients — without paying for every click.

## The TMS SEO Landscape

### High-Value Keywords
These searches signal real patient intent:
- "TMS therapy [city]" — someone looking for a clinic
- "TMS for depression near me" — ready to make a call
- "Does insurance cover TMS?" — evaluating whether they can afford it
- "TMS therapy cost" — comparing options
- "TMS vs [alternative]" — deciding between treatments
- "[Insurance carrier] TMS coverage" — checking their specific plan

### Competition Level
TMS SEO is moderately competitive. Hospital systems and large directories (including us) own the national searches. But **local searches** are often wide open. A well-optimized clinic website can land on page one for "[your city] TMS therapy" within 3–6 months.

## Local SEO Essentials

### Google Business Profile
This is the single most important thing you can do for local TMS visibility:
- Claim and verify your profile
- Work "TMS therapy" and "transcranial magnetic stimulation" into your business description
- Add real photos of your facility and treatment rooms
- Collect and respond to Google reviews
- Post updates regularly (weekly or biweekly)
- Make sure your address, phone, and hours are accurate

### Local Citations
Keep your clinic info consistent across:
- Google Business Profile
- Yelp
- Healthgrades
- Zocdoc
- Psychology Today
- TMS List (that's us)
- Your state medical board directory

Inconsistencies confuse Google and hurt your rankings.

### Service Pages
Build a dedicated page for each condition you treat:
- TMS for Depression in [City]
- TMS for OCD in [City]
- TMS for Anxiety in [City]

Each page targets a specific local + condition keyword.

## Content Strategy

### What to Write About
- Patient FAQs (cost, insurance, what to expect, side effects)
- Treatment guides for each condition
- Insurance-specific coverage guides
- Patient success stories (with permission)
- Your team's expertise and credentials

### Blogging Frequency
Quality beats quantity every time. One well-researched, 1,500+ word article per month does more than four thin posts. Write about the questions patients actually ask.

## Technical SEO

- **Fast page load** — under 3 seconds on mobile
- **Mobile-friendly design** — most healthcare searches happen on phones
- **Schema markup** — MedicalBusiness, Physician, and FAQPage structured data
- **SSL certificate** — non-negotiable for healthcare
- **Clean URL structure** — /tms-for-depression/ not /page?id=123

## Measuring Success

Track these monthly:
- Google Business Profile views and actions (calls, directions, website clicks)
- Organic search traffic to your site
- Keyword rankings for your target terms
- New patient inquiries from organic search
- Review volume and average rating
