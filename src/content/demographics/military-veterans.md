Veterans carry a disproportionate burden of depression, PTSD, and traumatic brain injury — conditions that tangle together and resist standard treatments. TMS is gaining ground in veteran mental health care, now available at a growing number of VA medical centers and veteran-focused clinics.

## What You'll Learn

- Why TMS is particularly effective for veterans
- VA TMS programs and how to access them
- TRICARE coverage for active duty and retirees
- TMS for veteran PTSD — current evidence
- Finding veteran-focused care

## Why TMS for Veterans

- **Treatment-resistant depression:** Many veterans have been through multiple medications without real relief
- **PTSD comorbidity:** Depression and PTSD almost always overlap. TMS can address both.
- **No medication burden:** When you're already on several medications, TMS adds zero drug interactions
- **TBI-related depression:** Post-TBI depression often responds poorly to medication but well to TMS
- **No sedation:** You can drive, work, and function after every session

## VA TMS Programs

The VA has been ahead of the curve on TMS:

- 20+ VA medical centers now offer it
- VA research has contributed significantly to TMS evidence, especially for PTSD
- If you're enrolled in VA healthcare, access TMS through an internal referral
- Community Care referrals are available if your local VA doesn't have TMS capability

## TRICARE Coverage

Active duty, retirees, and dependents covered under TRICARE can access TMS:

- Prior authorization is required
- You'll need 2+ documented medication failures documented in your record
- Available at both military treatment facilities and civilian network providers

## TMS for Veteran PTSD

TMS is FDA-cleared for depression, not PTSD — but the research in veteran populations is promising:

- VA-sponsored trials targeting right DLPFC for PTSD symptoms
- Deep TMS studies for combat-related PTSD showing results
- Many VA centers offer TMS for PTSD under research protocols
- Most veterans with PTSD also qualify for TMS under the depression indication — because the two almost always travel together
