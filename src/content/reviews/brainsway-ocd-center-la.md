## The Technology: Deep TMS and the H-Coil

Standard TMS devices (like NeuroStar) use a figure-8 coil that stimulates the brain's surface. The BrainsWay H-coil goes deeper. That's not marketing — it's physics.

### Why Deep TMS Makes Sense for OCD
- **The H7-coil** targets the anterior cingulate cortex and medial prefrontal cortex. These are the brain regions driving the OCD circuit — the ones that won't stop firing.
- **Depth:** Reaches roughly 3-4 cm below the skull, compared to 1.5-2 cm for standard figure-8 coils.
- **FDA clearance:** BrainsWay got specific FDA clearance for OCD in 2018, backed by a multicenter randomized controlled trial showing real Y-BOCS score reduction. Not "trends toward significance." Actual results.
- **Protocol:** 6 weeks of daily sessions, 29 sessions total. Each one includes something unique — a provocation exercise before stimulation.

### The Provocation — This Is the Interesting Part

Before each session, you spend 5-7 minutes being deliberately exposed to your personal OCD triggers. Sounds terrible, right? But there's strong evidence that stimulating the brain while the OCD circuit is already activated produces better outcomes.

The clinic's technicians are trained to develop and calibrate these provocations for each person individually. Most general TMS clinics simply don't have this skill set. It's a specialized craft.

## FAQ

### Does insurance cover TMS for OCD?
It's getting better. BCBS, UHC, and Cigna have started covering OCD TMS on many plans, but prior authorization is almost always required and denials happen more often than with depression TMS. The clinic's billing team handles appeals and reports about a 65% approval rate after the full appeal process. That means a third of people get stuck — so go in with your eyes open.

### How does deep TMS feel compared to regular TMS?
Broader and deeper. People describe a "thumping" sensation rather than the focused "tapping" of surface TMS. Most adjust within 2-3 sessions. The clinic can tweak coil positioning and comfort settings if you're struggling.

### Can I get TMS for both OCD and depression at the same time?
Yes. They treat both conditions in the same course using different coil configurations — H7 for OCD, H1 for depression. Sessions run a bit longer when you're treating both.
