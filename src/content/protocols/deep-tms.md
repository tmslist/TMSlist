## What is deep TMS?

Deep TMS (dTMS) uses a specialized H-shaped coil housed inside a padded helmet to deliver magnetic stimulation to deeper and wider brain regions than standard figure-8 coil TMS. Developed by BrainsWay, it's the primary alternative to standard rTMS.

## How H-coils work

Standard figure-8 coils focus stimulation on a small area (~2cm depth, ~1cm width). The H-coil creates a broader field pattern that reaches ~4cm depth and stimulates a larger volume of brain tissue. Different H-coil designs (H1, H7, etc.) are optimized for different brain targets.

## FDA-cleared indications

Deep TMS has the broadest FDA clearance profile of any TMS device:

| Condition | Year Cleared | H-Coil Type |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| Major Depressive Disorder | 2013 | H1 |
| Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder | 2018 | H7 |
| Smoking Cessation | 2020 | H4 |

## Protocol details

**For depression:**
- H1 coil targeting left DLPFC
- 18-20 minute sessions
- 36 sessions over 6-9 weeks
- 120% motor threshold intensity

**For OCD:**
- H7 coil targeting anterior cingulate and medial PFC
- 20 minute sessions
- Provocation paradigm (brief symptom exposure before stimulation)
- 29 sessions over 6 weeks

**For smoking cessation:**
- H4 coil targeting bilateral insula and PFC
- 18 minute sessions
- Combined with smoking cue exposure
- 18 sessions over 6 weeks

## Deep TMS vs standard rTMS for depression

Both achieve comparable response rates (50-60%) for depression. Deep TMS may have advantages for:
- Patients who haven't responded to standard rTMS (deeper targets)
- OCD (only FDA-cleared TMS option)
- Smoking cessation (only FDA-cleared TMS option)
- Conditions involving deeper brain structures (insula, medial PFC, anterior cingulate)

## The treatment experience

- Sit in a treatment chair
- Padded helmet placed over your head (contains the H-coil)
- Broader tapping/pulsing sensation than figure-8 coil (covers more scalp area)
- 18-20 minutes per session
- Some patients find the helmet less comfortable initially than a handheld coil
